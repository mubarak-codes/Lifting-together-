Lifting together is a non-profit organization that helps communities in Kenya achieve empowerment by increasing literacy for women and children, improving health status, and eradicating poverty.
The organization was started in August 2024 to provide shelter and clothing to orphans living on the outskirts of Nairobi, Kenya.
